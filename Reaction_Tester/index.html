Reaction Tester

Click the "Start" button to test your reaction time!

This game measures how quickly you can react when prompted. It's a fun way to test your reflexes and see how fast you can respond!

Rules and Regulations

  • Click the "Start" button to begin the test.
  • Wait for a random image of varying size and color to appear on the screen.
  • As soon as the image appears, click on it as fast as you can.
  • Delay in your click, Which increases the Reaction time.
  • Your reaction time will be displayed after the test.
  • Play in a distraction-free environment for accurate results.

How the Game Works

1. When you press "Start," a hidden timer will begin immediately.

2. An image of a random size and color will appear on the screen.

3. Click on the image as fast as possible to stop the timer and record your reaction time.

4. Try to improve your score or challenge your friends for the best reaction time!
